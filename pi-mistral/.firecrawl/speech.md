_Speech_

## **POST** /v1/audio/speech

Generate speech from text using a saved voice or a reference audio clip.

### Request Body

application/json

##### AdditionalProperties

map<any>

##### input

\*string

Text to generate speech from.

##### model

string\|null

##### ref\_audio

string\|null

The base64-encoded audio reference for zero-shot voice cloning.

##### response\_format

"pcm"\|"wav"\|"mp3"\|"flac"\|"opus"

##### stream

boolean

_Default Value:_`false`

##### voice\_id

string\|null

The preset or custom voice to use for generating the speech.

200 (application/json)

200 (text/event-stream)

Speech audio data.

##### audio\_data

\*string

Base64 encoded audio data

Response Type

[event-stream](https://docs.mistral.ai/api/endpoint/audio/speech#operation-speech_v1_audio_speech_post_responses_speechstreamevents) < [SpeechStreamEvents](https://docs.mistral.ai/api/endpoint/audio/speech#operation-speech_v1_audio_speech_post_responses_speechstreamevents) >

Speech audio data.

#### SpeechStreamEvents

{object}
